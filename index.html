About

My research examines how supervision and regulation shape financial stability, systemic risk, and innovation. I work on bank supervision, stablecoins, law and finance, and institutional design.

I am affiliated with the Centre for Business Law and Practice at Leeds and the Classical Liberal Institute at NYU Law. I am also a PhD candidate in Law and Economics of Money and Finance at Goethe University Frankfurt.
