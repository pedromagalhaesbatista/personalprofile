Published Articles

Columbia Journal of European Law (2024)

Good Administration in AI-Enhanced Banking Supervision: A Risk-Based Approach

Co-authored with Alessio Azzutti and Wolf-Georg Ringe. The article proposes a risk-based framework for using AI in banking supervision while protecting fundamental rights and preserving standards of good administration.

Stanford Journal for Blockchain Law and Policy (2021)

Dynamism in Financial Market Regulation: Harnessing Regulatory and Supervisory Technologies

Co-authored with Wolf-Georg Ringe. The article analyzes how RegTech and SupTech can make financial regulation more adaptive and dynamic, and discusses legal-institutional design choices for responsible deployment.
